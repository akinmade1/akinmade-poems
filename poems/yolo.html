YOLO

Published: July 1, 2025

“You only live once”

that line’s been echoing in my head for days.

And I wondered,

does that mean go wild?


Not wild like go fight a bear,

but wild like go face your fears.


Go ask her out

even if it ends in tears.

Because courage isn’t about the outcome,

it’s about the attempt.


Take that trip you’ve been thinking about.

No, it won’t ruin your simple life

it’ll expand it.

It’ll show you what life can offer,

what it means to be human.


Read that book you keep putting off.

Even if the pages confuse you

Isn’t that the point?

To learn?

And learning’s just like lifting weights:

the more you try,

the more it sticks.


So go.

Go wild.

Have fun.

Fall in love.

Fail hard.

Grow from it all.


That’s how you find out

what it really means

to be alive.


Because there’s no do-over.

— a.k
