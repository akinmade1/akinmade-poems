Why Worry?

Published: June 24, 2025

In 2125,

I’ll be nowhere —

Except my grave, if I’m lucky.

If not, maybe smoked — who knows? 😂


So why all the constant worry?

If that’s where it all ends anyway,

Why the shyness?

Why the fear?

Why all the what ifs and what if nots?


Why stress if the plan will work?

Why panic if you’re doing the “right thing”?

Is there even a perfect plan?


No one’s got the perfect way.

People will talk —

Right or wrong,

Loud or quiet,

They’ll always talk.


But in a hundred years,

No one will say,

“Remember that mistake he made?”

They’ll be gone too.


So why not drop the fears?

Drop the doubts.

Drop the trying to please everyone.


And live.


Because if you don’t,

There’ll be someone,

Somewhere,

Who’ll ask you one day:


Why didn’t you?

You
